I am an Assistant Professor at State Key Laboratory of Complex & Critical Software Environment, Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University, China. I have closely collaborated with Dr. Jiaheng Liu, Prof. Dong Xu, Prof. Wanli Ouyang, and Prof. Xianglong Liu.

Before that, I obtained my B.Eng (Hons1) degree from School of Electrical and Telecommunication, The University of New South Wales, Australia. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from School of Electrical and Information Engineering, The University of Sydney. My research interests lie in efficient and scalable AI computing (e.g., model-efficiency, data-efficiency, label-efficiency), neuromorphic computing (e.g., spiking neural network), AI4Science (e.g., AI for quantum computing).
