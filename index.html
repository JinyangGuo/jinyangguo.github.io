Jinyang Guo

Assistant Professor

Institute of Artificial Intelligence

Beihang University


I am an Assistant Professor at State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment, Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University, China. I have closely collaborated with Dr. Jiaheng Liu, Prof. Dong Xu, Prof. Wanli Ouyang, and Prof. Xianglong Liu.

Before that, I obtained my B.Eng (Hons1) degree from School of Electrical and Telecommunication, The University of New South Wales, Australia. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from School of Electrical and Information Engineering, The University of Sydney. My research interests lie in efficient and scalable machine/deep learning, including model-efficiency (e.g., model compression), data-efficiency (e.g., data compression), label-efficiency (e.g., few-/zero-shot learning).

[Prospective students] Our group has positions for PhD students, Master students, and visiting students. If you are interested, please send me an email with your CV and publications (if any).
